The Problem

Why most paid media plateaus

Platform algorithms are good, but they're optimizing for the platform, not your business. They'll happily spend your budget on low-intent traffic that looks good in dashboards but never converts.

The real issue: Most agencies treat paid media as a set-it-and-forget-it channel. They build campaigns, hit "launch," and check in monthly. Meanwhile, signal decays. Audiences shift. Creative fatigues. And performance drifts downward while you keep paying.

We take a fundamentally different approach: continuous signal extraction. Every impression, click, and conversion is a data point. We use ML to find patterns humans miss, then layer human judgment to act on them.

Our Approach
