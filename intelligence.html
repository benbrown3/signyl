Client Intelligence

Your dedicated AI analyst.

Every Signyl client gets a dedicated AI assistant that monitors your data and keeps your team informed. It surfaces the insights - our strategists decide what to do with them. The AI handles the monitoring so we can focus on the thinking.

Always Monitoring

Your assistant watches campaigns around the clock and alerts our team when something needs attention. We catch issues faster, so we can act faster.

Faster Insights

Instead of waiting for weekly reports, our team has real-time context on your account. That means faster decisions and more responsive strategy.

Read-Only Access

Your assistant can see your data but can't make changes. All optimizations go through our team. You stay in control, always.

How It Works

Context that builds over time.

1

Your dedicated AI connects to your marketing platforms (Google Ads, Meta, GA4, etc.) using secure OAuth. No passwords stored, full audit trail.

2

It learns your business

Over time, your assistant builds context about your goals, your audience, and what matters to you. It remembers past conversations and decisions.

3

Our team acts on insights

When the assistant surfaces an opportunity or flags an issue, our strategists evaluate it and take action. AI finds the signal, humans make the calls.
